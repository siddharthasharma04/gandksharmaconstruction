ENVIRONMENT, HEALTH, SAFETY POLICY

Our Responsibilities

  • To carry out the work with safety and health of personnel and properties in mind. To notify conditions that constitute hazard to those potentially affected.
  • To control the manner and methods of operations
  • To comply with applicable national, local EH&S legislation & regulations and ensure that employees receive proper training on the work to be done.
  • To demonstrate good work ethics without circumventing engineered safety devices and control.
  • To immediately inform Project Manager when an accident happened to its employee or Client personnel and properties. To submit a brief accident report within 24 hours and a detailed report within 72 hours.
  • No minors (below the age of Eighteen years) to be permitted to work on the Project site.
  • No kids / children to be permitted within the Project site.
  • To develop an emergency treatment and medical evaluation procedure. To set up a working arrangement with local medical doctor to handle injury cases, and provide for other treatment by a specialist or local hospital.
  • All persons must have Basic entry “awareness” of EH&S induction.

Smoking

  • Smoking is not allowed in the office & toilets.

P.P.E

To provide suitable personal protective equipment when the job requires. As Per Risk Analysis type and quality of PPE will be recommended for use. This includes: -

  • Hardhat with chinstrap.
  • Leather safety shoe with steel toe
  • Face Shield to be used during welding.
  • Goggles during gas cutting, drilling, grinding, overhead works.
  • Hand gloves as per risk analysis
  • Earplugs for works at more than 85db noise level.
  • Nose mask in dust, fumes, smoke and gas.
  • Full body harness with double lanyard of 1.5m length and snap hook of 50mm diameter for all works at height
